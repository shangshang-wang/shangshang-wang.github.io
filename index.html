Shangshang Wang 王上上

 

I am a final-year Computer Science (CS) Master's student at the Intelligence, Information and Decision (IID) Lab, part of the Network Intelligence Center (NICE), which is housed within the School of Information Science and Technology (SIST) at ShanghaiTech University, China. My current advisor is Prof. Ziyu Shao. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from ShanghaiTech as well.

My current research focuses on ‘‘Constrained Bandit Learning for Networked Systems’’ and encompasses

  • (Theory) re-assessment of bandit learning methods, specifically considering operational constraints like budget and fairness.

  • (Application) implementation of such refined methods to bolster networked systems, including federated learning and crowdsourcing systems, etc.
